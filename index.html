About Me

Developer. Creator. Problem Solver.

I'm a full-stack developer with a passion for immersive technologies and social experiences. My work spans across web development, backend systems, mobile applications, and game development - with a particular focus on VR and XR platforms.

As Co-founder and Director of VR at PepperCastXR, I help bring virtual performers to live events using cutting-edge display technologies. I'm also an active contributor to open-source projects like BasisVR, where I work on improving avatar systems and network performance.
