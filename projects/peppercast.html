PepperCastXR / VRIRL

A turn-key solution for immersive virtual talent, bringing virtual performers from their homes and studios to live events worldwide.

Role: Co-founder / Director of VR Status: Active

About PepperCastXR

PepperCastXR provides cutting-edge technology to bring virtual performers performing remotely from the comfort of their own houses and studios directly to your event. We leverage a variety of display technologies including holofans, LCD screens, and specialized projection fabrics commonly used in vocaloid concerts to create truly immersive live experiences.

Our platform bridges the gap between virtual performers and physical venues, enabling a new era of entertainment where geography is no longer a barrier to live performance.

Technologies

PepperCastXR combines entertainment industry standards with cutting-edge VR technology to deliver seamless virtual performances.

VRChat

Primary platform for virtual performer capture and interaction, supporting cross-platform experiences across PC VR, standalone VR, and desktop.

DMX Lighting Control

Industry-standard DMX512 protocol for synchronized lighting manipulation at live venues and clubs, ensuring virtual performers integrate seamlessly with physical production.

Holographic Displays

Holofan technology and specialized projection fabrics (pepper's ghost effect) used in professional vocaloid concerts for lifelike virtual presence.

Backup Platform Infrastructure

Redundant worlds built for NeosVR and BasisVR ensuring continuity if the primary VRChat platform experiences issues.

Live Production Pipeline

Real-time video capture, streaming, and compositing systems to bring virtual performers into physical event spaces with minimal latency.

Multi-Platform World Development

Cross-platform world creation supporting PC VR, Quest standalone, and desktop users simultaneously in a single unified experience.

VRChat SDK & Udon

Deep expertise in VRCSDK, Udon, and UdonSharp (U#) for creating interactive world logic, networked systems, and custom behaviors.

VRSL & Lighting Systems

VRSL (VR Stage Lighting) for DMX-controlled lighting in VRChat, along with VRC Light Volumes for advanced volumetric lighting effects.

AudioLink & VRCDN

AudioLink integration for audio-reactive visuals and effects. VRCDN for reliable video streaming and content delivery within VRChat worlds.
