Cheeks' VPM Registry

My Unity packages for VRChat, installable straight from the VRChat Creator Companion. Add the registry once and every package (and update) shows up in the VCC automatically.

Add to VCC View index.json
https://vpm.cheeksy.dev/index.json

The Add to VCC button needs the VRChat Creator Companion installed — your browser will ask to open it.

Manual Setup

If the one-click button doesn't work, adding the registry by hand takes under a minute:

  1. Open the VRChat Creator Companion and go to Settings → Packages.
  2. Click Add Repository, paste https://vpm.cheeksy.dev/index.json, and confirm.
  3. Open any project's Manage Project screen — my packages now appear in the list, ready to install.
